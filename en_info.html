Can I bring a guest?

Please check your invitation to see if you may bring an additional guest.

Will the ceremony and reception take place at the same venue?

Yes! Both events will take place at Casa Quintal.

Is there a dress code for the wedding?

We won’t have a dress code or required colors. We just want everyone to feel comfortable and celebrate with us ✨

Where can I park?

On the day of the event, you may park at Stillos Parking, located next to the venue.

Any recommendations for where to stay in São Paulo?

We recommend staying near the venue, in neighborhoods such as Consolação, Jardins, Higienópolis, or Pinheiros for easier transportation.

Where can I get ready in São Paulo?

São Paulo offers many options, and several beauty salons are open on Sundays, such as "Espaço A+", "Studio W Higienópolis", and "Fast Escova Higienópolis", among others.
